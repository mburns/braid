Getting Help Online

While we'd love for you to pay us to do it, the Twisted developers are likely to be online and able to answer any questions you might have regarding the framework. Realize, before you make any unreasonable demands through these channels, that there are people who are paying some of us for this, and so we don't have to take any crap from you for free :-).

Real-time help is available with Internet Relay Chat. Likely hangouts are #twisted and #twisted.web (for web-related Twisted-based technologies) on the FreeNode Network.

If you have any patience, you can increase the chances that your question is heard by using the general discussion mailing list and/or the twisted-web mailing list. Please subscribe first, then post a query about your problem; we will try to answer promptly. The list is relatively low in traffic.
